East African Cuisine

Known for its diverse flavors and the use of spices like ginger, nutmeg, and coriander, East African cuisine features a wide variety of dishes that reflect the culture and traditions of the region.

Cooking Techniques

East African cooking techniques often involve grilling (such as in Nyama Choma), steaming, and frying. Stews and curries are also common, often cooked slowly to develop rich, deep flavors. Traditional cooking is usually done in one-pot dishes, making the process simple and efficient.

Signature Dishes

  • Mandazi
  • Ugali
