West African Cuisine

West African cuisine is rich, hearty, and full of flavor. Meals are often centered around starchy staples like cassava, plantains, or rice, paired with spicy sauces and stews. Known for its bold use of spices such as ginger, nutmeg, pepper, and cloves, this region's food is both comforting and exciting.

Cooking techniques in West Africa include slow simmering, grilling, and frying. Soups and stews are essential, often thickened with nuts or seeds.
